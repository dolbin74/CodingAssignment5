Ingredients

  • 2 yellow onions eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 to 2 dashes Tabasco sauce, to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup milk

Equipment

  • small saucepan
  • tablespoon
  • fork
  • knife
  • platter

Directions

  1. Season chuck roast generously with salt and pepper. Rub enough
  2. Rub enough flour over the roast to evenly coat the surface.
  3. In a large skillet pan, warm oil over high heat until very hot.
  4. Sear the roast until browned and crusty on all sides, about 10 minutes total.
  5. Place roast in the pot of a slow cooker. Pour beef broth around the roast, then sprinkle with seasoning mix, top with butter
  6. Scatter pepperoncini over and around the roast.
  7. Cover the slow cooker and set on low heat for 7 to 8 hours. When ready, shred the meat, mixing with the sauce in the slow cooker
  8. Serve over bread, egg noodles, or potatoes.
